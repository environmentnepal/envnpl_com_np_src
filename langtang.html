Langtang National Park

Established: 1976

Location: Rasuwa, Nuwakot, Sindhupalchok

Area: 1,710 km²

Entry Fees: NPR 100 (Nepali), NPR 1500 (SAARC), NPR 3000 (Foreign)

Best Season to Visit: October to November, March to May

Wildlife

Red panda, Himalayan black bear, snow leopard, wild dog.

How to Reach

7-8 hour drive from Kathmandu to Syabrubesi.

About Langtang National Park

Langtang National Park is one of Nepal's protected areas, offering a unique ecosystem and wildlife experience. The park is managed by the Department of National Parks and Wildlife Conservation (DNPWC) and plays a crucial role in Nepal's conservation efforts.

All entry fees and details are subject to change. Check with official sources before visiting.
