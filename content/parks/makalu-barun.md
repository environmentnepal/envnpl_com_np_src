# Makalu Barun National Park

**Established:** 1992

**Location:** Sankhuwasabha, Solukhumbu

**Area:** 1,500 km²

**Entry Fees:** NPR 100 (Nepali), NPR 1500 (SAARC), NPR 3000 (Foreign)

**Best Season to Visit:** October to November, March to May

## Wildlife

Snow leopard, red panda, clouded leopard, Himalayan tahr.

## How to Reach

45-min flight Kathmandu to Tumlingtar, then drive to Num.

## About Makalu Barun National Park

Makalu Barun National Park is one of Nepal's protected areas, offering a unique ecosystem and
wildlife experience. The park is managed by the Department of National Parks and
Wildlife Conservation (DNPWC) and plays a crucial role in Nepal's conservation
efforts.
