# Bardiya National Park

**Established:** 1988

**Location:** Bardiya

**Area:** 968 km²

**Entry Fees:** NPR 100 (Nepali), NPR 750 (SAARC), NPR 1500 (Foreign)

**Best Season to Visit:** October to March

## Wildlife

Bengal tiger, one-horned rhinoceros, Gangetic dolphin, swamp deer.

## How to Reach

Flight from Kathmandu to Nepalgunj, then 2-hour drive.

## About Bardiya National Park

Bardiya National Park is one of Nepal's protected areas, offering a unique ecosystem and
wildlife experience. The park is managed by the Department of National Parks and
Wildlife Conservation (DNPWC) and plays a crucial role in Nepal's conservation
efforts.
