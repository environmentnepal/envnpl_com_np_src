# Shey Phoksundo National Park

**Established:** 1984

**Location:** Dolpa, Mugu

**Area:** 3,555 km²

**Entry Fees:** NPR 100 (Nepali), NPR 1500 (SAARC), NPR 3000 (Foreign)

**Best Season to Visit:** May to October

## Wildlife

Snow leopard, blue sheep, Tibetan wolf, Himalayan griffon.

## How to Reach

Flight Kathmandu-Nepalgunj-Juphal, then 2-day trek to park HQ.

## About Shey Phoksundo National Park

Shey Phoksundo National Park is one of Nepal's protected areas, offering a unique ecosystem and
wildlife experience. The park is managed by the Department of National Parks and
Wildlife Conservation (DNPWC) and plays a crucial role in Nepal's conservation
efforts.
