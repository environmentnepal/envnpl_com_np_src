# Chitwan National Park

**Established:** 1973

**Location:** Chitwan, Nawalparasi, Parsa, Makwanpur

**Area:** 952.63 km²

**Entry Fees:** NPR 150 (Nepali), NPR 1000 (SAARC), NPR 2000 (Foreign)

**Best Season to Visit:** October to March

## Wildlife

Bengal tiger, one-horned rhinoceros, gharial, Asian elephant, sloth bear.

## How to Reach

5-hour drive or 25-min flight from Kathmandu to Bharatpur.

## About Chitwan National Park

Chitwan National Park is one of Nepal's protected areas, offering a unique ecosystem and
wildlife experience. The park is managed by the Department of National Parks and
Wildlife Conservation (DNPWC) and plays a crucial role in Nepal's conservation
efforts.
