# Rara National Park

**Established:** 1976

**Location:** Mugu, Jumla

**Area:** 106 km²

**Entry Fees:** NPR 100 (Nepali), NPR 1500 (SAARC), NPR 3000 (Foreign)

**Best Season to Visit:** April to May, September to November

## Wildlife

Himalayan black bear, red panda, musk deer, leopard.

## How to Reach

Flight from Nepalgunj to Talcha Airport, then 3-hour walk.

## About Rara National Park

Rara National Park is one of Nepal's protected areas, offering a unique ecosystem and
wildlife experience. The park is managed by the Department of National Parks and
Wildlife Conservation (DNPWC) and plays a crucial role in Nepal's conservation
efforts.
