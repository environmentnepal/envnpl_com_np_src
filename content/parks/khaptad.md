# Khaptad National Park

**Established:** 1984

**Location:** Bajhang, Bajura, Doti, Achham

**Area:** 225 km²

**Entry Fees:** NPR 100 (Nepali), NPR 500 (SAARC), NPR 1500 (Foreign)

**Best Season to Visit:** March to May, October to November

## Wildlife

Leopard, Himalayan black bear, wild boar, yellow-throated marten.

## How to Reach

Flight from Kathmandu to Dhangadhi, then 8-hour drive to Silgadhi.

## About Khaptad National Park

Khaptad National Park is one of Nepal's protected areas, offering a unique ecosystem and
wildlife experience. The park is managed by the Department of National Parks and
Wildlife Conservation (DNPWC) and plays a crucial role in Nepal's conservation
efforts.
