# Sagarmatha National Park

**Established:** 1976

**Location:** Solukhumbu

**Area:** 1,148 km²

**Entry Fees:** NPR 100 (Nepali), NPR 1500 (SAARC), NPR 3000 (Foreign)

**Best Season to Visit:** October to November, March to May

## Wildlife

Snow leopard, Himalayan tahr, red panda, musk deer.

## How to Reach

35-min flight from Kathmandu to Lukla, then trek.

## About Sagarmatha National Park

Sagarmatha National Park is one of Nepal's protected areas, offering a unique ecosystem and
wildlife experience. The park is managed by the Department of National Parks and
Wildlife Conservation (DNPWC) and plays a crucial role in Nepal's conservation
efforts.
