# Shuklaphanta National Park

**Established:** 1976

**Location:** Kanchanpur

**Area:** 305 km²

**Entry Fees:** NPR 100 (Nepali), NPR 750 (SAARC), NPR 1500 (Foreign)

**Best Season to Visit:** October to March

## Wildlife

Swamp deer (largest herd in world), Bengal tiger, elephant, hispid hare.

## How to Reach

Flight Kathmandu to Dhangadhi, then 1.5-hour drive.

## About Shuklaphanta National Park

Shuklaphanta National Park is one of Nepal's protected areas, offering a unique ecosystem and
wildlife experience. The park is managed by the Department of National Parks and
Wildlife Conservation (DNPWC) and plays a crucial role in Nepal's conservation
efforts.
