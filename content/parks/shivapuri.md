# Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park

**Established:** 2002

**Location:** Kathmandu, Nuwakot, Sindhupalchok

**Area:** 159 km²

**Entry Fees:** NPR 100 (Nepali), NPR 600 (SAARC), NPR 1000 (Foreign)

**Best Season to Visit:** September to May

## Wildlife

Leopard, Himalayan black bear, barking deer, wild boar.

## How to Reach

30-min drive from central Kathmandu to Budhanilkantha or Sundarijal.

## About Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park

Shivapuri Nagarjun National Park is one of Nepal's protected areas, offering a unique ecosystem and
wildlife experience. The park is managed by the Department of National Parks and
Wildlife Conservation (DNPWC) and plays a crucial role in Nepal's conservation
efforts.
