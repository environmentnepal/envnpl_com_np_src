# Banke National Park

**Established:** 2010

**Location:** Banke, Dang, Salyan

**Area:** 550 km²

**Entry Fees:** NPR 100 (Nepali), NPR 750 (SAARC), NPR 1500 (Foreign)

**Best Season to Visit:** October to March

## Wildlife

Bengal tiger, one-horned rhinoceros, striped hyena, four-horned antelope.

## How to Reach

Flight Kathmandu to Nepalgunj, then 1-hour drive.

## About Banke National Park

Banke National Park is one of Nepal's protected areas, offering a unique ecosystem and
wildlife experience. The park is managed by the Department of National Parks and
Wildlife Conservation (DNPWC) and plays a crucial role in Nepal's conservation
efforts.
