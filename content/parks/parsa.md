# Parsa National Park

**Established:** 1984

**Location:** Parsa, Bara, Makwanpur

**Area:** 637.37 km²

**Entry Fees:** NPR 100 (Nepali), NPR 750 (SAARC), NPR 1500 (Foreign)

**Best Season to Visit:** October to March

## Wildlife

Bengal tiger, Asian elephant, sloth bear, striped hyena.

## How to Reach

3-hour drive from Kathmandu or 15-min from Birgunj.

## About Parsa National Park

Parsa National Park is one of Nepal's protected areas, offering a unique ecosystem and
wildlife experience. The park is managed by the Department of National Parks and
Wildlife Conservation (DNPWC) and plays a crucial role in Nepal's conservation
efforts.
